Core Architecture

One pipeline. Raw signal in, a safe route out.

Three automated layers translate raw satellite sensor files into a live, navigable mission map.

INPUT

Chandrayaan‑2 Data

DFSAR radar + OHRC optical imagery

LAYER 01

Volumetric Ice Detection Core

CPR > 1.0 & DOP < 0.13 → dielectric modelling → ice volume in m³

LAYER 02

Morphological Hazard Mapping

Slope > 15° and obstacles > 0.5 m masked as no-go zones

LAYER 03

Thermal-Aware Route Engine

A*/D* Lite "Shadow Hopping" with solar charging pitstops

OUTPUT

Mission Control Dashboard

Live ice, hazard & route layers + LMRS score

Volumetric Estimation Core

Estimates ice depth and volume down to 5 metres using dielectric modelling on DFSAR data — pure dust sits near ε≈2.5, ice mixtures shift to ε≈3.5.

Terrain Hazard Masking

Uses DEMs and OHRC imagery to flag terrain that's physically dangerous: slopes beyond 15° and boulders or micro-craters larger than 0.5 m.

Mission Copilot

Predictive Battery Model

Dynamic regression models estimate battery discharge profiles and solar charge durations.

09

Confidence Overlay

Dielectric signal-to-noise confidence overlay to quantify measurement uncertainties.

10

Budget-Constrained Mission Optimizer

Set an energy or cost ceiling and the system automatically searches every candidate site and route, recommending the single best mission plan that fits inside that budget — with a one-line explanation of why it won over the runner-up.

11

Failure-Mode Replanning (Anomaly Injection)

Inject a simulated fault — like a wheel actuator degrading mid-traverse — and watch the energy-aware pathfinder automatically replan to the nearest safe or charging waypoint in real time, with updated ETA and risk.

12

AI Mission Briefing

Feeds LMRS, hazard, and ice data into an LLM to auto-generate a plain-English mission note explaining why a site was chosen, in seconds.

13

Lunar Day/Night Time-Lapse

Animates solar illumination sweeping across a crater rim over a full lunar day, showing how safe solar-charging pitstop windows shift over time.

14

Offline-First PWA Mode

Caches the last loaded mission data so the dashboard keeps working fully even if the network drops mid-demo.

15

Exportable Mission Report

One-click PDF or CSV export of a selected site's ice volume, hazard summary, and route plan, styled like a formal mission packet.
